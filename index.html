A bit about James.

James D. McDougald Jr. is an entrepreneurial minded individual, striving to create a legacy for himself and his family.

Mr. McDougald is seeking a position as a junior developer in which he can focus on the front-end development. With a student mentality, James has his sights on not just finding a job, but starting a career in which he can be provided structure to learn more as well as apply his creativity. His experience in sales and marketing give him a competitive edge in understanding the demands and needs of the end-user.

He is driven by his desire to learn as he pursues a career as a front-end developer. He finds gratification in overcoming the seemingly impossible obstacles, as well as conquering daily goals.

Back in 2016 James had an idea for an innovative app revolving around his favorite hobby, basketball. After doing much research, he then realized, with commitment and hard work, he could learn how and build the app himself. He then immediately began using several online courses to teach himself the basics, but this was only the start. Having a strong desire for knowledge & determination to build upon his programming abilities led him to take on a 10 week training course at codecademy. During this time he actively progressed and improved in his capabilities. Since then, with assistance, he has developed and maintained a few projects of his own.

During his short time living in Germany, he has made it his mission to learn the language & culture. He successfully completed B2 qualification in the german language and integration course at the bildungsverein Hannover. But he would not stop there, he then participated in a three-month seminar in which attendees were strengthened in their basic german skills. He remains nevertheless a strong believer that the best way to learn the language and most things in life is by immersing himself in situations where he is required to learn. "Diamonds are created only under intense pressure", he would say everytime asked about this topic.
